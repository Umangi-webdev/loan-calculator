## 📌 About

A **Loan Calculator** built with **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript** that helps users calculate their monthly **EMI (Equated Monthly Installment)**, total interest payable, and total repayment amount based on the loan amount, interest rate, and loan tenure.

The application provides instant results through a clean and intuitive interface, making loan planning simple and efficient.

- 💰 Calculate Monthly EMI

## 🧮 How It Works

1. Enter the **Loan Amount**
2. Enter the **Annual Interest Rate**
3. Enter the **Loan Duration**
4. Click the **Calculate** button
5. View:
   - Monthly EMI
   - Total Interest
   - Total Payment
